# 100 V13 Concept Mapping Inventory V1

## Mission
- Freeze the first bounded concept-mapping framework for `V1.3`.

## In Scope
- mapping modes for concept stocks
- source-quality layering
- point-in-time mapping rules
- market-confirmation requirements

## Out Of Scope
- direct news ingestion
- direct strategy signals
- model training

## Success Criteria
- Freeze a reusable concept-mapping inventory that future bounded concept seeds can consume.

## Stop Criteria
- If the inventory cannot separate source truth, concept mode, and market confirmation, the branch should not widen.

## Handoff Condition
- After this inventory, the next legal action is a bounded concept-seed rather than wider ingestion or model work.
