# V1.12U CPO Foundation Completeness And Research-Readiness Review

This phase exists to answer one narrow owner-level question after the `V1.12Q/R/S/T` chain:

- is the current CPO information foundation already good enough for bounded deep research?
- and separately, is it already good enough for formal training?

The phase is intentionally judgmental rather than expansive. It does not add new cohort rows, new chronology anchors, or new spillover rows. It only reviews whether the already-frozen foundation is structurally strong enough for the next lawful research layer.

Boundaries:

- in scope:
  - review of schema hardening, adjacent cleanup, chronology normalization, and spillover truth-check
  - explicit remaining-gap listing
  - explicit separation of research-readiness and training-readiness
- out of scope:
  - training
  - feature promotion
  - signal generation
  - execution logic
  - automatic phase expansion
