# V1.12AH Factor Candidate Preservation Rule

- Purpose:
  - stop the research line from over-cleaning potentially valuable factor candidates
  - preserve speculative, overlay, blind-spot, and pending structures until they are truly disproved
- Core principle:
  - candidates may be downgraded or archived
  - candidates may not be silently deleted just because they are weak, noisy, or not yet promotable
- Immediate use:
  - this rule applies to the next owner review after `V1.12AG`
