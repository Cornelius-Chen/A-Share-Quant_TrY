# V1.13A Theme Diffusion State Schema V1

- `V1.13A` now records theme diffusion in four separate layers instead of one mixed narrative bucket:
  - phase state
  - stock role
  - mainline strength
  - context-backed driver
- This phase also freezes an **open candidate-driver layer**:
  - unknown or newly discovered strong-mainline drivers may be searched by subagents
  - but they remain `review-only candidate drivers`
  - they do not enter formal schema law automatically

## Review-Only Candidate Drivers

- high priority:
  - `policy_backing_tier`
  - `industrial_advantage_alignment`
  - `market_regime_tailwind`
  - `event_resonance_intensity`
- medium priority:
  - `leader_height_establishment`
  - `mid_core_confirmation`
  - `cross_day_breadth_diffusion`
  - `absorption_quality_or_a_kill_suppression`
  - `catalyst_freshness_and_reinforcement`
- deferred:
  - `mapping_clarity_and_tradeable_story`

## Reading

- The project now has a proper language for:
  - why a line becomes a mainline
  - why some lines hold through pullback and others A-kill
  - why some themes can spread into laggards and relays while others remain one-stock spikes
